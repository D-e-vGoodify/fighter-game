Welcome to My Fighting-game

I just want to tell you how the buttons of the game work

  • Once a button is tapped, the players move in the direction and the don't stop on their own but they need to be stopped by the "stop" button.
  • The attack button unleashes the sword to attack the enemy.
  • Be very careful not to allow your player get lost outside the screen.
  • If the players get lost outside the screen, they can still be brought back in using the buttons
  • This game is a two player game and is not a vs computer game but a person can still play it alone for fun
